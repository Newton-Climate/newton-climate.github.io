Coding packages:

SpectralFits.jl

I designed and implemented this flexible interface to simulate concentrations of greenhouse gas molecules corresponding to a variety of observation instruments. I used a bayesian inversion algorithm to infer statistical measures such as the Shannon information content, degrees of freedom, and the posterior error covariance. This work resulted in 2 invited talks and 1 publication.

OHMethane

I designed this model to simulate atmospheric methane chemistry and infer global eissions based on chemical constraints. Like SpectralFits.jl, this model uses a bayesian algorithm to infer climate-relevant emissions including methane, carbon monoxide, and other species. This work resulted in 2 conference presentations and 2 publications.
